Source Data & Methodology

The Filter:

We do not report on "announced" projects or vapourware. This list includes only ventures with verifiable commercial signals in 2025–2026:

  • Significant Funding Events (Seed/Series A)
  • Verifiable Incorporation or Commercial Launch
  • Credible Institutional Backing (e.g., Y Combinator, a16z, Rally Ventures)

Exclusions: Legacy companies founded 2020–2024 were removed to isolate the current AI-native structural shift.
